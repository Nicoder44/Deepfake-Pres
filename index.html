Deepfakes

An Introduction to Deep Learning-based Fake Media

What are Deepfakes?

Deepfakes are AI-generated media that replace a person's likeness with someone else's.

Examples

  • Face-swapped videos
  • Voice cloning
  • Text-to-speech synthesis

How Deepfakes Work

Deepfakes use deep neural networks to analyze and generate realistic media.

Risks and Concerns

  • Misinformation
  • Privacy breaches
  • Impersonation

Detection and Mitigation

Efforts to detect and combat deepfakes are ongoing.

Conclusion

Deepfakes present both exciting possibilities and serious challenges for our society.
